Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Membership

What is Founding Membership?

Founding Members are the first members of The Marque Club, joining during our establishment phase. They receive lifetime privileges, preferred rates, and the distinction of helping shape our legacy.

How many Founding Members will be accepted?

We are limiting Founding Membership to preserve exclusivity. Exact numbers vary by location but represent less than 30% of our eventual total membership.

What are the membership requirements?

We seek accomplished individuals - HNI's, CEO's, CXO's etc - who embody excellence in their fields and share our values of integrity, sophistication, and community. Professional achievement, personal character, and cultural appreciation are all considered.

Can I visit before applying?

Private tours are available to qualified applicants during the application process.

Is membership transferable?

Founding Memberships include transferable rights, subject to approval.

Facilities & Services

What dining options will be available?

Each location will feature a fine dining restaurant, casual lounge, private dining rooms, and a bar. Menus will be crafted by celebrated chefs with both international and regional influences. We also offer a premium Cigar Club for connoisseurs.

Can I bring guests?

Yes. Members may bring guests in accordance with club policies. Founding Members receive enhanced guest privileges.

Will there be accommodation?

Select locations will offer limited guest suites for members and their guests.

What wellness facilities are available?

We offer spa facilities designed for complete rejuvenation and relaxation.

Do members get airport lounge access?

Yes. Members enjoy exclusive access to premium private lounge at Hyderabad Airport.

Are there age restrictions?

The club is designed for adults. Specific policies regarding children will be established at each location.

General

When will the first location open?

We anticipate our first location to open in 2026. Founding Members will be notified first and invited to preview events.

Will all locations be identical?

Each Marque Club will reflect the character of its city while maintaining our uncompromising standards. Core amenities will be consistent; local touches will be distinctive.

Do you offer corporate memberships?

We offer select corporate partnership opportunities. Contact partnerships@themarqueclub.com for details.

Still Have Questions?

Our Membership Team is here to assist you with any inquiries.
